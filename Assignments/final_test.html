Location Found: Laurel Park

The paper that started this collection. Walking down to my apartment this paper caught my eye in the gutter by the park. Something about the combination of the art, text, and handwriting made me decide to keep it.

EREN Stickers

Location Found: Various bus stop shelter walls.

What went from a single piece of paper became a real collection when I started finding EREN stickers around town. It gave me the idea to collect interesting papers to try and create what became known as the 'House Bible'.

Fresno Criminal Court Scam

Location Found: My front porch

My grandma has been recieved a call about me being in jail in Fresno and needing $6500 for some reason or another, with instructions to go to Fred Meyer to transfer money. She called my father who reassured her that I was not trapped in Fresno jail waiting for her to help me. However, she still insisted on visiting the apartment and confirming my safety. I made sure to take the paper she brought so she wouldn't follow up on anything. I had already planned on including it in the collection before I even saw the back side of the sheet...

Local Hero's Gift

Location Found: My apartment stairwell

When leaving my apartment there was a vagrant laying down on the platform between the first and second floor. He greeted me and said he was just here to use the light to draw. Not thinking much of it, I stepped over him and went about my day. 4 hours later upon my return, the man was still there laying and drawing so like before I stepped over and continued on. In the morning, that spot was trashed with broken pens and ciggarette butts. All that was left was this piece of paper.
